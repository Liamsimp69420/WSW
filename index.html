About us

WSW was founded after our CEO, William, saw a watch that his friend had pulled apart, the watch had its dial pulled off and William fell in love with the design.

Using this 'no-dial' design William designed the company's first watch: the William Severin One. The William Severin One sported blue hands and a blue and white striped NATO strap.

Later that year, WSW released the William Severin Two, the William Severin Two features red hands and a Khaki strap while keeping the theme of not having a dial similar to the William Severin One.

Now the company makes all sorts of watches tailored to the customer's taste and is currently working on a sporty chronograph.
